Bidmachine

Bidmachine is a smart contract bid evaluation engine designed to help organizations score, rank, and audit supplier proposals automatically. It produces transparent, explainable results, flags anomalies, and generates downloadable audit reports for compliance.

Use Case: A city government issues an RFP for road maintenance and needs a reliable, unbiased evaluation of all bids submitted.
