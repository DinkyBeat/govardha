Bidmachine

Bidmachine is a contract bid evaluation engine designed for organizations to automatically score, rank, and audit supplier proposals. It produces explainable results, flags anomalies, and generates downloadable audit reports for compliance purposes.

Use Case: A city government issues an RFP for road maintenance and needs unbiased evaluation of all bids.
