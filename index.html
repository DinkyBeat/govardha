Featured Project

Bidmachine

Bidmachine is a contract bid evaluation engine that automatically scores and ranks supplier proposals using transparent, configurable criteria. It generates explainable results, flags anomalies, and produces a full audit trail to support fair and accountable procurement decisions.

Use case example: A city government issues an RFP for road maintenance.
