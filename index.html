Bidmachine
Bidmachine is an advanced contract bid evaluation engine that automatically scores, ranks, and audits supplier proposals. The system ensures full transparency and accountability, flags potential anomalies, and generates downloadable audit reports for compliance purposes. Its modular design allows organizations to adapt scoring criteria to their specific requirements, making it ideal for government and corporate procurement processes.
